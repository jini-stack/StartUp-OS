Mission Statement

What is a mission statement? Your mission defines your company's purpose and why you exist today. It should be clear, concise, and actionable.
Example: "To organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful." - Google
Tip: Keep it to 1-2 sentences. Focus on the problem you solve and who you serve.

Vision Statement

What is a vision statement? Your vision describes the future you want to create. It's aspirational and describes where you're headed in 5-10 years.
Example: "To be Earth's most customer-centric company, where customers can find and discover anything they might want to buy online." - Amazon
Tip: Think big and inspirational. What does success look like?
